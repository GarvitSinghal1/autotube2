Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Autotube collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our local video automation tool.

1. Information We Access

Autotube uses the YouTube Data API v3 to automate uploading videos to your YouTube channel. When you authenticate Autotube, you grant us access to the following scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.upload: Allows the application to manage and upload your videos to YouTube.

2. Data Security & Local Storage

Your privacy is paramount. Autotube is designed as a locally run script. This means:

  • No External Storage: We do not run any central database, cloud server, or tracking system. All of your credentials (including Client ID, Client Secret, and Refresh Token) are stored on your local machine in your configuration files (e.g., .env).
  • No Data Sharing: Your Google Account details, videos, and credentials are never transmitted to us or any third party (other than directly to Google/YouTube APIs during the upload process).

3. Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Autotube's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can open an issue on our project's public GitHub repository.
